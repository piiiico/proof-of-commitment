# Privacy Policy — Proof of Commitment

**Last updated: April 3, 2026**

## What We Collect

The Proof of Commitment extension collects **anonymous behavioral data** — website domains you visit and time spent — for the sole purpose of generating aggregate trust signals for AI agents and researchers.

### Data collected locally (on your device):
- Website domains visited
- Time spent per domain
- Visit counts per domain

### Data submitted to our backend (only when you choose to submit):
- Domain name
- Visit count
- Total time spent (in seconds)
- First and last visit timestamps (within a 7-day window)

## What We Do NOT Collect

- **No personal identifiers** — no name, email, IP address, or user ID
- **No browsing history** — only the specific domains you have visited, not URLs or page content
- **No cross-domain tracking** — submissions are unlinkable across different businesses
- **No location data**
- **No financial information**

## Identity Verification (World ID)

We use World ID (Worldcoin) to verify that each contributor is a unique human. When you sign in:
- We verify you are a unique person (one person = one account)
- Your World ID credential is **never sent to our servers**
- Verification happens locally in your browser
- You remain anonymous — we cannot identify who you are

## Data Aggregation

Submitted data is aggregated by domain. We store only aggregate statistics:
- Number of unique verified contributors
- Total visits across all contributors
- Repeat visit rate
- Average time spent

This aggregated data is made available via an API so AI agents can query real behavioral signals instead of relying on potentially fake reviews.

## Data Retention

- Local data is stored in your browser and cleared after submission
- Aggregated backend data is retained indefinitely as it is anonymous
- You can clear your local data at any time by uninstalling the extension or using Chrome's extension storage clear feature

## Third-Party Services

- **World ID (Worldcoin):** Used for proof-of-personhood verification. Subject to [Worldcoin's Privacy Policy](https://worldcoin.org/privacy-policy).
- **Cloudflare Workers:** Our backend infrastructure. Subject to [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, we cannot identify you to fulfill data deletion requests. All data is anonymous by design.

## Changes

We will post any changes to this policy in the extension's Chrome Web Store listing.
