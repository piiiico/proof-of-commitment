# Chrome Web Store Listing — Proof of Commitment

## Extension Name
Proof of Commitment

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Prove your loyalty to businesses — anonymously. Cryptographically verified behavioral data that AI agents can query instead of fake reviews.

## Full Description (16,000 chars max)

**Content is free to fake. Commitment is not.**

Reviews are gamed. Ratings are manufactured. Any content-based signal can be faked at scale with AI.

But real behavioral patterns cannot be faked cheaply. A person who visits the same restaurant 12 times over a month is a fundamentally stronger signal than a thousand five-star reviews — harder to fake, structurally honest.

**Proof of Commitment replaces opinion with behavior.**

---

### How It Works

1. **Browse normally** — the extension passively tracks which domains you visit and how much time you spend there
2. **Verify you're human** — sign in with World ID to prove you're a unique person (not a bot)
3. **Submit anonymously** — send your behavioral signals with no identity attached
4. **Your commitment counts** — your data joins an aggregate that AI agents can query: "How many real humans are loyal customers of this business?"

---

### What Makes This Different

**Anonymous by design** — we never store your identity. World ID proves you're human without revealing who you are.

**Zero surveillance** — no URLs, no page content, no cross-domain tracking. Only: domain, visit count, time spent.

**Cryptographically verified** — each data point is backed by proof of personhood. One human, one contribution.

**AI-queryable** — the aggregate signals are available via MCP (Model Context Protocol) so AI assistants can query real behavioral trust instead of relying on fake reviews.

---

### What We Collect (and What We Don't)

✅ Website domains visited
✅ Time spent per domain (approximate)
✅ Visit frequency

❌ No URLs or page content
❌ No personal information
❌ No IP addresses
❌ No browsing history linkage
❌ No financial data

---

### Permissions Explained

- **storage**: Save your local behavioral data before submission
- **tabs / activeTab**: Detect which domain you're currently visiting
- **identity**: Enable World ID sign-in flow
- **alarms**: Schedule periodic data cleanup
